Solution

The plan to bring the farmers' market back to life.

Breakdown

The situation was clear, unlike the weather: the farmers market lost business because the schedule was dependent on the weather. A lack of a proper communication stream was revealed to be the problem because the main factor in confusion was miscommunication about whether the event was to be hosted.

So we stepped in to offer a plan to install the missing stream.

Semantics

The website would work to be the passive online presence of the market, to act as the reliable hub of necessary information. If a knowing consumer ever had a question pertaining to the market, they know where to find the answers.

The newsletter would act as the aggressive informer, contacting both old and potential new consumers about the market, what would be there, the location, and of course the website address itself.

Application

Through this two step process, the market creates a two point communication stream between it and its customers. The newsletter acts to remind the lost individual of the status of the event, while the website has all the details.

Reception


While the newsletter contacts the customers, a customer can check the website at their own discretion.

The advantage of this system to the populace is that they do not need to put any effort in involuntarily to get their information. The newsletter does the work sending information, and only specific information requires any more work, which would be a simple click of the link.
